LISTEN LAUNCH EXHIBITION

The third edition of Rogue entitled 'Listen' encompassed a diverse group of female creatives addressing the personal ways that the epidemic of Gender-Based Violence in South Africa affects their lives. The realities of GBV affect us all in different ways, but a good place to start addressing these issues is to create a space where individuals feel that they are safe to speak up and to let them be heard. To Listen, and then to act.

Rogue sought to encompass all of these factors, by creating a space, listening, and acting. With the help of The META Foundation and The TEARS Foundation, we hosted an exhibition of the artist's works and created a safe environment that encouraged the start of a conversation. Through this exhibition, a portion of all profits was donated to TEARS, an organization that fights to protect women and children who have been victims of violence. We managed to donate R2700 and hope to continue raising awareness for this amazing foundation and important cause.
