FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I place an order?

On the navigation bar, click magazines, select 'order' under the issue you want to order and we'll be in touch

What will a copy cost me?

For digital access to the magazine, it will cost R150.00, if you'd like a hard copy delivered to you it will cost R200.00 plus delivery costs.

How often are the releases?

Releases are every 3 months, so keep an eye out for the next one coming your way.
